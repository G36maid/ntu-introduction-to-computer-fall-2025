## The Technology Industry
Nearly every job requires interaction with technology to complete projects, exchange information, and meet customer needs. Organizations look for employees who are comfortable using technology and desire to learn.

## Information Systems in the Enterprise

**Information System**: A set of hardware, software, data, people, and procedures that work together to produce information.

### Qualities of Valuable Information
To assist with sound decision making, information must be:
*   **Accurate**
*   **Verifiable**
*   **Timely**
*   **Organized**
*   **Accessible**
*   **Useful**
*   **Cost effective**

### Enterprise Functional Units
A typical enterprise consists of departments/divisions:
*   Human Resources (HR)
*   Engineering or Product Development
*   Manufacturing
*   Marketing
*   Sales
*   Customer Service

### Types of Information Systems

#### Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)
Integrates information flow across an organization to manage and coordinate ongoing activities:
*   Product planning
*   Manufacturing and distribution
*   Accounting and finance
*   Sales
*   Human resources
*   Customer support

#### Document & Content Management
*   **Document Management System (DMS)**: Storage and management of company documents (access control, security, version tracking).
*   **Content Management System (CMS)**: Manages publishing, modification, and organization of content (webpages, files).

#### Transaction Processing System (TPS)
Captures and processes data from day-to-day business activities.
*   **OLTP (On-Line Transaction Processing)**: Transactions are processed immediately (real-time).
*   **Batch Processing**: Transactions are collected and processed at a later time.

#### Management Information System (MIS)
Generates accurate, timely, and organized information so managers can make decisions, solve problems, and track progress. (e.g., Detailed Flight Reports).

#### Decision Support System (DSS)
Helps users analyze information and make decisions.
*   **OLAP (On-Line Analytical Processing)**: Analysis of complex data.

#### Expert System
Captures and stores the knowledge of human experts and imitates human reasoning and decision making (e.g., Restaurant Advisor).

## Technology Careers

### Career Areas
*   Management
*   Research and software development
*   Technical support services
*   Operations
*   Training/Support
*   Information security services
*   Marketing/Strategy

### Career Fields
*   **Equipment**: Manufacturers/distributors of hardware (IBM, Cisco, etc.).
*   **Software and Apps**: Developers of OS, applications, etc.
*   **Service and Repair**: Preventive maintenance, component installation.
*   **Sales**: Requires general tech understanding and specific product knowledge.
*   **Education/Training**: Corporate trainers, professors.
*   **IT Consulting**: Providing expertise to clients.

### Specific Job Roles

#### System Development
*   **Cloud Architect**: Cloud storage/services strategies.
*   **Cognitive Engineer**: AI-based machines.
*   **Database Designer**: Structure/interface of databases.
*   **Program/App Developer**: Design/implement code.
*   **Systems Analyst**: Analyzes user requirements, designs systems.
*   **Web Developer/Designer**: Website functionality and appearance.

#### Technology Operations
*   **Computer Technician**: Hardware/software install and repair.
*   **Help Desk Specialist**: Answers tech questions.
*   **Network Administrator/Engineer**: LANs/WANs config and maintenance.
*   **Technical Project Manager**: Guides design/dev tasks.

#### Web Marketing & Social Media
*   **CRM Specialist**: Customer Relationship Management.
*   **SEO Expert**: Search Engine Optimization.
*   **UX Designer**: User Experience.

#### Data & Analysis
*   **Data Scientist**: Analytics/Big Data.
*   **Database Administrator (DBA)**: Maintains data dictionary, monitors performance.
*   **Digital Forensics Examiner**: Analyzes evidence.

#### Security
*   **Security Analyst**: Implements security procedures.
*   **Network Security Administrator**: Configures firewalls/routers.
*   **Computer Security Specialist**: Data security responsibility.

#### Mobile & App Development
*   **Mobile Strategist**: Mobile initiatives.
*   **VR Engineer**: Virtual Reality applications.
*   **Game Designer/Programmer**.
